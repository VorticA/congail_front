About

We are Congail - an Irish-Bulgarian band from Plovdiv. Our art is comprised of Chaos and Moonlight, we tell tales of ancient Goddesses, evil spirits, magical creatures and more...



Origin


The band was created by Maebh, Brenos and Aoibhe Mac Gonagle who are classical musicians.Later drummer Stoil Bozhilov, guitarist Nikola Lulov and flutist Javor Angelov joined and the sound of the band was formed.



Music


Their music is a kind of protest against the stagnation and fear of creation, implanted in the minds of classical musicians. It incorporates various elements, from classical to jazz, swing, punk and even metal.
